# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
- **CEO**: Checks 2-3x daily between meetings. Needs instant clarity on company health. 30-second glances. State of mind: busy, decisive, needs confidence that things are handled.
- **VP of Product (Mykola)**: Primary power user. Multiple times daily. Deep dives into problems. State of mind: stressed but driven, needs to see what's broken and track fixes.
- **Head of Engineering (Oleksii)**: Checks sprint health, team bottlenecks, bug density. State of mind: analytical, wants facts not opinions.
- **VP of Media Buying (Alexander)**: Campaign performance, ROI, buyer efficiency. State of mind: commercially focused, needs money numbers fast.

All users are Ukrainian-speaking executives in a media buying company (~100 people). They use this dashboard in meetings (screen sharing), on laptops, and occasionally on phones.

### Brand Personality
**Calm. Precise. Authoritative.** Like a premium medical instrument — you trust it because it's clearly well-made, shows exactly what matters, and doesn't waste your attention.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Reference**: Vibrant Wellness by Noomo Agency (vibrant.noomoagency.com) — soft pastel gradients, frosted translucent cards, premium feel, ethereal quality
- **Theme**: Light primary (soft lavender/blue gradient background), dark mode available via toggle
- **Frosted glass**: YES — this is our design identity. Override impeccable's glass ban for this project.
- **Font**: NOT Plus Jakarta Sans (banned by impeccable). Find a distinctive alternative that fits "calm, precise, authoritative."
- **Color restraint**: Mostly neutral. Color ONLY for status (green = good, red = bad, amber = watch). No decorative color.
- **Anti-references**: Generic SaaS dashboards. Grafana. Dark mode with neon accents. Cookie-cutter card grids.

### Design Principles
1. **Information density over visual flourish** — Every pixel earns its place through content, not decoration
2. **Status through color, hierarchy through size** — Red/green/amber for status; large/small for importance
3. **One glance, one answer** — The homepage answers "what needs attention?" without any interaction
4. **Drill-down, not navigate** — Click any metric to understand WHY, not to go to another page
5. **Calm authority** — Professional enough for a board meeting. No anxiety-inducing design.
